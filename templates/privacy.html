Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

1. Data We Collect

When you create an account with Spendly, we collect the information you provide directly, such as your name and email address. As you use the Service, we also collect the expense records you enter, including amounts, categories, dates, and descriptions.

2. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used solely to provide and improve the Service. Specifically, we use it to display your expense history, generate summaries and reports, and maintain your account. We do not use your financial data for advertising, profiling, or any purpose beyond delivering Spendly to you.

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored securely on our servers. We take reasonable technical and organisational measures to protect it against unauthorised access, loss, or disclosure. You may request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time by contacting support.

4. Third-Party Services

Spendly does not sell or share your personal data with third parties for their own purposes. We may use trusted third-party infrastructure providers (such as hosting or database services) strictly to operate the Service. These providers are bound by confidentiality obligations and are not permitted to use your data independently.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or how your data is handled, please contact us at support@spendly.app. We will respond to all reasonable requests within a reasonable timeframe.
